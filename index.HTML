French Toast

This is the way of making a French toast with simple ingredients in less than 5 mins!

Ingredients

  • Toasts 5
  • eggs 2
  • Cup of milk 3/4
  • Salt 1/2 SS
  • Sugar 1 BS
  • Cinnamon 1 SS

Instructions

  1. Crack 2 eggs in an empty pan
  2. Clean the eggs from the unwanted whites
  3. Add the milk
  4. Add the sugar and cinnamon and salt
  5. Stir the mixture properly
  6. dip the toast in the mixture well from both side
  7. in a clean pan heat up some butter
  8. grill the toast until it get a caramelly color and smell
  9. ENJOY!
